ABOUT US

The Explorers is a photography club initiated by the students of University of Petroleum and Energy Studies, Dehradun. The Explorers is a platform for beginners & photography enthusiasts which organize beginner photography workshops, photo walks, outdoor trips, exhibi- tions to help them express the creative side! We are always attempting to remove all the potential explanations behind you to not take up photography. The motive behind making this group is to have all the like-minded photography lovers at one place. Collectively, we aspire to grow as a photography community and keep learning in the process. At the Explorers, we envisage to continue organizing different events ranging from Photo Exhibitions, Photo walks, Photography competitions, and much more in the University and in Dehradun.
